# Kinora Product Vision

**Version:** 0.2

**Status:** Draft

**Last Updated:** 30 June 2026

**Author:** Aparna J S

## Vision

Kinora is a mobile-first family coordination platform that helps families plan, organize, and collaborate in one place.

Our goal is to replace scattered information across WhatsApp chats, spreadsheets, notebooks, and phone calls with a single, simple, and reliable application that keeps everyone informed and organized.

The first version of Kinora focuses on event planning, enabling families to manage guests, vendors, tasks, purchases, and event information with confidence. Over time, Kinora will evolve into a modular platform supporting other aspects of family life such as medical records, habits, documents, and more.

## Success Criteria

When using Kinora, families should feel that:

* Planning is simpler and less stressful.
* All important information is available in one place.
* Everyone involved has access to the latest information.
* Tasks and responsibilities are clear.
* Guest counts and event details remain accurate.
* Time spent searching through chats and spreadsheets is significantly reduced.

## Product Principles

* Mobile-first experience.
* Simple and intuitive for every family member.
* If a feature cannot be explained to a non-technical family member in under one minute, it's probably too complicated for the MVP.
* Modular architecture that grows over time.
* Privacy and security by design.
* Solve real family problems before adding advanced features.
* Progress over perfection.
